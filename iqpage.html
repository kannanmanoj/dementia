What is an IQ Test?

An IQ test is a standardized assessment tool used to measure a person's cognitive abilities and problem-solving skills. It evaluates various aspects of intelligence, including logical reasoning, verbal comprehension, spatial awareness, and mathematical ability. IQ tests are widely recognized as valuable tools for assessing intellectual potential and guiding personal and professional development.
